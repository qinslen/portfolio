Gear Design • 3D Printing

Design & Fabrication of a 3D‑Printed Gearbox

Designed, simulated, and fabricated a functional gearbox with a 1:12 speed ratio using additive manufacturing. Focus areas included optimizing gear meshing, reducing friction, and validating a robust mechanism suitable for engineering applications. The work emphasizes practical creativity in problem‑solving and translating design theory into manufacturable outcomes.

CAD • Simulation

Design & Simulation of a CNC Router in SolidWorks

Conceived and modeled a CNC router, applying mechanical design principles to achieve reliable operation for prototyping use.
